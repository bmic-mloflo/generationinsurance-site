SMS Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 13, 2026

At Generation Insurance, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use and protect your personal information, specifically regarding our SMS communication services.

Information We Collect

We collect your name, phone number and policy details when you voluntarily provide them to us through our intake processes, email correspondence or verbal consent.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide insurance quotes, policy updates, renewal reminders and customer support.

Sharing of Information (SMS Compliance)

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All other categories exclude text messaging originator opt‑in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your data from unauthorized access, as required by the Florida Information Protection Act (FIPA).

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us at processing@generationinsurance.net.
