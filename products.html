Products & Services

Customized coverage to meet your immediate and future needs

Our staff of dedicated insurance professionals cultivates an insurance program personalized to your needs. As your life changes, we constantly monitor those changes to ensure you're protected with the best possible coverage now and for the years ahead.

Homeowners Insurance

Homeowners insurance compensates you for losses to your home and possessions inside it. It provides added security for your investment and protects you if you're legally liable for someone's injuries on your property, as well as from financial losses caused by storms, fire, theft and other events outlined in your policy.

Dwelling Fire

Dwelling fire policies are for homes rented to others. They protect your house, garage, sheds or other structures from damage caused by fire, collapse, explosion, hail, lightning, smoke, vandalism or wind. If you own a home and rent it out, many homeowners policies won't cover damage to a home you don’t live in; a dwelling fire policy fills this gap.

Condo/Townhome Unit Owner's

As a condo or townhome owner, it's important to insure not only your personal possessions but also any built‑in units such as cabinets, fixtures, appliances and shelves. In addition to covering personal property, a condo owner needs liability coverage. Your association's master policy generally covers the building's structure; our specialized policy covers the rest.

Renters Insurance

If you rent your home, renters insurance protects your personal property from events like fire, theft and liability suits. It ensures you're compensated for losses while giving you peace of mind.

Auto Insurance

We help you find affordable auto coverage that fits your needs and budget, ensuring you meet state requirements while providing additional protection for your vehicle and passengers.

Motorcycle Insurance

Whether you ride a sport bike, cruiser or touring bike, our specialized motorcycle policies provide protection for your machine, safety apparel and equipment—and include roadside assistance to keep you moving.

Boat Insurance

Our boat policies cover various watercraft—from small fishing boats to yachts. They include coverage for towing, total loss replacement and more, allowing you to enjoy the water with confidence.

Flood Insurance

Homeowners insurance does not cover flood damage. Flood insurance is available to anyone living in a community that participates in the National Flood Insurance Program. We help you evaluate whether you need flood coverage and find the right policy.
