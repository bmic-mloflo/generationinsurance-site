SMS Terms of Service

Program Description

Generation Insurance provides one‑to‑one SMS messages to clients for insurance‑related purposes, including policy status updates, document requests and renewal notifications.

User Opt‑In

By providing your mobile number and giving verbal or written consent, you agree to receive text messages from Generation Insurance. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

Message Frequency & Cost

Message frequency varies based on your policy activity. Standard message and data rates may apply from your carrier.

How to Opt‑Out

You can cancel the SMS service at any time. Simply text STOP to our business number. After you send the message “STOP” to us, we will send you a confirmation message to verify that you have been unsubscribed.

How to Get Help

If you experience issues with the messaging program, reply with the keyword HELP for more assistance, or contact us directly at processing@generationinsurance.net.

Florida Disclaimer

Please note that insurance coverage cannot be bound, altered or cancelled via text message. All policy changes must be confirmed in writing or via a recorded call with a licensed agent.
